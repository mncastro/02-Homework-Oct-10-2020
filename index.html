Bio

Latin American Studies PhD. Full-Stack Web Developer. Humanities researcher and translator. Monterrey born. Adopted by Texas.


I've worked as an educator, researcher, and translator for almost ten years. During that time, I've earned academic degrees in Mexico and the United States, published book reviews, and academic essays in English, and worked as an English teacher for private companies. I'm interested in Digital Humanities, databases, and online archives. My doctoral research focuses on Mexico's Dirty War and the legacies of urban guerrillas of the 1970s and 1980s.


Looking for academic faculty positions, freelance translation, and coding opportunities.
